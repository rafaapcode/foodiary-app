## üì± About the App

Foodiary allows you to log your meals in two convenient ways:
- **üì∏ Photo**: Take a picture of your meal and let AI identify the foods

- Daily progress tracking towards your goals
- Organized history of all your meals
- Visual statistics of your nutritional intake
